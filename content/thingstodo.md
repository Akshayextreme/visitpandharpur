# Things To Do in Pandharpur

Apart from visiting the temple, there are several activities visitors can enjoy.

### Visit Chandrabhaga River Ghats
Pilgrims take a holy dip before visiting the temple.

### Attend Temple Aarti
Morning and evening aartis are a powerful spiritual experience.

### Explore Local Markets
Markets sell religious items, souvenirs, and devotional books.

### Try Maharashtrian Food
Enjoy dishes like:

- Puran Poli
- Sabudana Khichdi
- Traditional Thali
