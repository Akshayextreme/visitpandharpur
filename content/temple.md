# Vitthal Rukmini Temple

The Vitthal Rukmini Temple is the spiritual heart of Pandharpur.

### Temple Timings

- Opening: 4:00 AM
- Kakad Aarti: 4:30 AM
- Mahapuja: 11:00 AM
- Evening Aarti: 7:00 PM
- Closing: 11:30 PM

### Visitor Tips

- Visit early morning
- Expect heavy crowds during Ekadashi
- Carry water during summer
