# How to Reach Pandharpur

### By Train
Pandharpur railway station connects to cities like Solapur and Pune.

### By Road
Regular buses run from Pune, Mumbai, and Solapur.

### Nearby Cities

- Solapur – 70 km
- Pune – 210 km
- Mumbai – 360 km
