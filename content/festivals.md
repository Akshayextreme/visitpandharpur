# Festivals in Pandharpur

Pandharpur is famous for its religious festivals attracting millions of devotees.

### Ashadhi Ekadashi

The biggest festival where the **Wari pilgrimage** reaches Pandharpur.

Devotees walk hundreds of kilometers carrying palkhis of saints.

### Kartiki Ekadashi

Another major pilgrimage day with huge crowds.

### Maghi & Chaitri Ekadashi

Smaller but important temple celebrations.

### Travel Tips

- Expect very large crowds during Ashadhi Ekadashi
- Book accommodation early
- Visit early morning for darshan
